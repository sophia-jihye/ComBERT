 ITEM 1. BUSINESS. 

J.W. Mays, Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148; or &#147;Registrant&#148;) with executive offices at 9 Bond Street, Brooklyn, New York 11201, operates a number of commercial real estate properties, which are described in Item 2 &#147;Properties&#148;.
