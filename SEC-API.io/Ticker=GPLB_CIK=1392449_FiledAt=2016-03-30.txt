 ITEM 1. BUSINESS 

Description of Business 

Green Planet Bioengineering Co., Ltd. (&#8220;Green Planet&#8221; or &#8220;Company&#8221;) now operates as a public reorganized shell corporation with the purpose to acquire or merge with an existing business operation. 

1. General Information 

Mondo Acquisition II, Inc. was incorporated in the State of Delaware on October 30, 2006 and the name was changed to Green Planet Bioengineering Co., Ltd. (&#8220;Company&#8221;) on October 2, 2008. In October 2008, the Company acquired Elevated Throne Overseas Ltd, incorporated in British Virgin Islands, and its subsidiaries (&#8220;Elevated Throne&#8221;) and operated the business in the agritech sector in the People&#8217;s Republic of China. The Company divested Elevated Throne to One Bio, Corp. (&#8220;ONE&#8221;) on April 14, 2010. 

In March 2012, the Company became a wholly owned subsidiary of Global Funds Holdings Corp. (&#8220;Global Funds&#8221;) an Ontario, Canada corporation. 

The Company operates as a public reorganized shell corporation with the purpose to acquire or merge with an existing business operation. The Company's activities are subject to significant risks and uncertainties, as their ability to implement and execute future business plans and generate sufficient business revenue is directly influenced by their ability to secure adequate financing or find profitable business opportunities.
